Principal's Message

With more than 60 years of legacy in delivering high-quality education, Somaiya Vidyavihar provides a wide range of academic programs across various disciplines. Founded in 2019, Somaiya Vidyavihar University holds the distinction of being the first private university in Mumbai, and one of its significant offerings is the Engineering education, through K. J. Somaiya College of Engineering (KJSCE), Mumbai.
